Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat shipping fee of $5.99 applies.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund.

Are all products certified organic?

Yes! All our products are 100% certified organic and meet USDA organic standards. We work only with certified organic farmers and suppliers.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee at checkout.
